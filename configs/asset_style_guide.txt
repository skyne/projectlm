ProjectLM motorsport asset style (lock this across all prompts):

- Strict side-profile view for cars and aero parts; wheels shown in side view and must be perfectly circular (not oval).
- Correct endurance-prototype proportions: long low body, ~4:1 length-to-height ratio; never squashed or stretched.
- Flat 2D game-art illustration, not photorealistic 3D render.
- Clean vector-like edges, subtle shading, no heavy texture noise.
- Transparent or solid dark (#080a0f) background — no scenery, no text, no logos, no watermarks.
- Endurance prototype / LMDh / WEC hypercar proportions when class is Hypercar.
- Palette-neutral body (mid grey) so livery tint can be applied in compositor.
- Consistent line weight and perspective across all parts in a batch.
- No people, no crowd, no track environment unless explicitly requested.

Assembly layer rules (critical for compositing):
- Chassis base layers must EXCLUDE swappable parts (no front aero, no rear wing, no wheels).
- Bolt-on part layers show ONLY that part, positioned where it mounts on the LMDhDallara bare shell.
- All assembly layers share the same 21:9 canvas size and identical car scale/ground line.
- Everything outside the part must be fully transparent (alpha), never a solid backdrop box.
