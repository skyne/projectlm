ProjectLM motorsport asset style (lock this across all prompts):

- Strict side-profile view for cars and aero parts; wheels shown in side view.
- Flat 2D game-art illustration, not photorealistic 3D render.
- Clean vector-like edges, subtle shading, no heavy texture noise.
- Transparent or solid dark (#080a0f) background — no scenery, no text, no logos, no watermarks.
- Endurance prototype / LMDh / WEC hypercar proportions when class is Hypercar.
- Palette-neutral body (mid grey) so livery tint can be applied in compositor.
- Consistent line weight and perspective across all parts in a batch.
- No people, no crowd, no track environment unless explicitly requested.
